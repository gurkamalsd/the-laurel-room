Meet the Founder

Khushbu Dhahan

I built The Laurel Room because I was tired of the same problem you have.

Every time my family needed a beautiful space for 80 guests — a mehndi night, a baby shower, a milestone birthday — there was nothing. Oversized banquet halls that forced us into their catering. Community centres with fluorescent lights and folding chairs. Or a 45-minute drive to Toronto for something that actually looked good in photos.

So I'm building what should have existed all along: an intimate, stunning venue right here in Brampton where you bring your own food, your own music, your own traditions — and walk into a room that's already beautiful.
