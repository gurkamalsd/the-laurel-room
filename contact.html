FAQs

Common Questions

What does BYOC mean? +

Bring Your Own Caterer. You choose any caterer you want — we provide the venue, styling, and kitchen facilities for warming and plating. Serve halal, vegetarian, Caribbean, Filipino, or any cuisine your family loves.

Can we serve alcohol? +

Yes. Clients arrange a Special Occasion Permit (SOP) through the AGCO. A Smart Serve-certified server is required. We can guide you through the process.

What's included in the styled packages? +

Everything you see in the photos: linen tablecloths, cloth napkins, charger plates, styled centerpieces, a statement backdrop arch, LED uplighting in your chosen preset color, fairy light canopy, and full professional setup and teardown.

How far in advance should we book? +

We recommend booking 3–6 months in advance for weekend dates, especially during peak season (September–December). Weekday dates are often available on shorter notice.

Is the venue accessible? +

Yes. The venue is on the ground floor with step-free access, accessible parking, and accessible washrooms.
